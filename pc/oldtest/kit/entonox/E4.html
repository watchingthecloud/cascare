Pipe and Patient Demand Unit

 

This is connected to the Entonox at the valve on the side of the cylinder head. It is clicked into position and once the cylinder is switched on, with a filter and mask or mouth piece attached, it is ready for use by the patient.

 

Once use of the entonox is finished or the cylinder runs out, the cylinder should be closed off and patient demand unit should be purged by pressing the button on the back of the unit once. The pipe can then be released by a simple twist of the white valve collar at the point of attachment to the cylinder.
